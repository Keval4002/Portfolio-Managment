PORTFOLIO ML DATASET DESCRIPTION
================================

DATASET OVERVIEW:
Comprehensive stock market dataset covering 24 companies across 4 major sectors,
plus S&P 500 benchmark data for portfolio analysis and ML model training.

DATA SCOPE:
- Time Period: 5 years (2015-2020)
- Total Records: 1,259 daily observations per stock
- Companies: 24 stocks + S&P 500 benchmark
- Features per Stock: Date, Close Price, Volume
- Total Raw Features: 51 columns

SECTOR BREAKDOWN:

1. AVIATION SECTOR (6 companies):
   - AAL (American Airlines)
   - ALGT (Allegiant Travel Company)
   - ALK (Alaska Air Group)
   - DAL (Delta Air Lines)
   - HA (Hawaiian Airlines)
   - LUV (Southwest Airlines)

2. FINANCE SECTOR (6 companies):
   - BCS (Barclays)
   - CS (Credit Suisse)
   - DB (Deutsche Bank)
   - GS (Goldman Sachs)
   - MS (Morgan Stanley)
   - WFC (Wells Fargo)

3. HEALTHCARE SECTOR (6 companies):
   - BHC (Bausch Health Companies)
   - JNJ (Johnson & Johnson)
   - MRK (Merck & Co.)
   - PFE (Pfizer)
   - RHHBY (Roche Holding)
   - UNH (UnitedHealth Group)

4. TECHNOLOGY SECTOR (6 companies):
   - AAPL (Apple)

   - IBM (IBM)
   - MSFT (Microsoft)

5. BENCHMARK:
   - SP500 (S&P 500 Index)

FEATURE ENGINEERING:
From the raw 51 columns, the system generates 89 advanced portfolio-level features:

1. PORTFOLIO AGGREGATIONS (15 features):
   - Total portfolio value, average price, median price
   - Price standard deviation and variance
   - Portfolio returns (current, lagged 1-3 periods)
   - Moving averages (5, 10, 20 day) and ratios
   - Portfolio volatility metrics

2. TECHNICAL INDICATORS (12 features):
   - RSI (Relative Strength Index)
   - Bollinger Bands (upper, lower, position)
   - MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence)
   - Price momentum and trend indicators

3. SECTOR ANALYSIS (32 features - 8 per sector):
   For each sector (Aviation, Finance, Healthcare, Technology):
   - Total value and average price
   - Price standard deviation
   - Total trading volume
   - Sector returns and lagged returns
   - Sector moving averages (5, 10 day)
   - Sector volatility (5-day rolling)

4. MARKET COMPARISON (8 features):
   - S&P 500 price, returns, volume
   - Portfolio vs S&P 500 return differential
   - Portfolio vs S&P 500 value ratio
   - Beta calculation (30-day rolling)
   - Correlation with market (30-day rolling)

5. CROSS-SECTOR CORRELATIONS (12 features):
   - Pairwise correlations between all sector combinations
   - Rolling 30-day sector correlation analysis

6. VOLUME AND LIQUIDITY METRICS (10 features):
   - Total portfolio volume
   - Volume-weighted metrics
   - Liquidity indicators
   - Volume momentum and trends

TARGET VARIABLE:
- Next-day portfolio total value (regression target)
- Calculated as sum of all 24 companies' closing prices
- Used for predicting portfolio performance

DATA QUALITY:
- ✅ No missing values after preprocessing
- ✅ Consistent time alignment across all stocks
- ✅ Proper handling of weekends and holidays
- ✅ Volume data normalized and validated
- ✅ Outlier detection and treatment applied

DATA SPLITTING:
- Training Set: 982 samples (80%) - 2015 to Oct 2019
- Test Set: 246 samples (20%) - Oct 2019 to Sep 2020
- Validation: 20 rolling windows for cross-validation
- Temporal ordering preserved (no data leakage)

DATA USAGE:
This dataset enables the ML models to:
1. Learn portfolio-level patterns and relationships
2. Capture sector dynamics and correlations
3. Understand market cycles and trends
4. Generate accurate portfolio value predictions
5. Support personalized investment recommendations

The comprehensive feature engineering transforms raw stock data into meaningful
portfolio insights, enabling sophisticated ML analysis for investment decisions.
