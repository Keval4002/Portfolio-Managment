Q11. How many models were tested and what were they?

     ENSEMBLE METHODS: Random Forest Regressor, Gradient Boosting, XGBoost

Q12. What validation methodology was used and why?
A12. 20 ROLLING WINDOWS CROSS-VALIDATION:
     ‚Ä¢ Initial window: 200 days, Step size: 50 days
     ‚Ä¢ Maintains temporal ordering (prevents data leakage)
     ‚Ä¢ Each window: 80% training, 20% testing
     ‚Ä¢ No future data used to predict past (crucial for time series)
     ‚Ä¢ Rigorous validation across 20 different time periods

Q13. What were the exact train/test splits?
A13. ‚Ä¢ TRAINING: 80% (2015-2019) = 982 samples
     ‚Ä¢ TESTING: 20% (2019-2020) = 246 samples
     ‚Ä¢ Temporal split maintains chronological order
     ‚Ä¢ Cross-validation: 20 rolling windows with same 80/20 ratio
